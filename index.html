Pokemon Type Calculator

Repository Live Project

Pokemon is one of Nintendo's most popular and well known game series, among other titles such as Mario and Zelda. However, Pokemon tends to attract a younger audience due to the simplicity of the story and mechanics: gather a team of 6 magical creatures to battle your way through the region and become champion. There are plenty of damage calculators online for Pokémon, but these are typically aimed at more advanced players, giving so much information it would overload a beginner. I was inspired to build a simple, quick, and easy web app that would allow even children to be able to understand how to use it.
